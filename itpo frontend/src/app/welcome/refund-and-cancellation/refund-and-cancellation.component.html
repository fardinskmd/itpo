Once the user has purchased the ticket(s) and paid the fee online through payment gateway, he/she will not be able to cancel the booking under any circumstances. No requests for cancellation/refund will be entertained for such cases

In the event there is any claim for/ of charge back by the customer for any reason whatsoever, such customer shall immediately contact ITPO with his/ her claim details. Any such request has to be sent through email to “info[at]itpo[dot]gov[dot]in” from the registered email address used while purchasing the entry ticket(s).
